BLT Recipe

Quick, delicious meal for busy times.

  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonaisse
  • Avocado
  1. Cut tomato and avocado into thin slices
  2. Fry bacon
  3. Spread mayonaisse onto toasted bread
  4. On toast, add avocado, lettuce, tomato, and 4 slices of bacon
  5. Enjoy!
