Japanese Curry Recipe

Wholesome curry dish with a Japanese sweet twist.

  • Oil
  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Curry Mix
  • Honey
  • Apple
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ketchup
  1. Slice chicken andvegetables into small pieces
  2. Finely dice apple, garlic, and ginger
  3. Add 2 tbsp oil to hot pan
  4. Once hot, add garlic, ginger and onion to pan and cook for 1 min
  5. Now add chicken and cook until outside is no longer pink
  6. Add potatoes and carrots to pan and toss for a minute or 2
  7. Pour enough water to cover carrots + potatoes and set to high heat until boil
  8. Let simmer for 20 mins or until carrots and potatoes soften
  9. Turn off heat and disolve curry mix one brick at a time
  10. Put on medium-high heat and add apple, and 1 tbsp honey
  11. Now add 1 tbsp soy sauce and 1 tbsp ketchup
  12. Plate and serve with rice
