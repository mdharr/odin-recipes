Overnight Oats Recipe

Easy, healthy breakfast for everyone.

  • Old-fashioned Oats
  • Almond Milk
  • Chiaseeds
  • Yogurt
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Maple Syrup
  1. Pour 1/2 cup of old-fashioned oats into mason jar
  2. Add 1 tbsp chia seeds and 1 tbsp maple syrup
  3. Pour 1/2 cup almond milk and mix
  4. Add with 1/2 cup yogurt
  5. Top with strawberries and blueberries
  6. Let sit overnight or 8 hours for best results
